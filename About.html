TANILISED WOOD AKA GREEN TIMBER

Timber being a natural product has unique properties and these can vary for species to species. Most of our products are made from Tanilised pine. Tanilising is a high pressure treatment used to protect timber from insert infestions and fungal rot. This treatment pentrates deep into the wood and can last up to 60 years for internal products and 30 years for external products.


Tanilising uses a combination of copper and biocides, with the copper giving the green colour, which is why this type of treatment is often refered to as "Green Timber"
