THOR JOINERY

For all your Bepsoke Joinery needs.

Based in King Cross, Halifax, we are an open workshop that not only manufactures a whole array of garden and household furniture solutions, but is also a place where you can visit to discuss any joinery project that you may have.

We also carry out commercial projects of every kind. We have time served bench joiners of the highest quality and have undertaken work ranging from heritage renovations to bespoke solutions.

Our work encompasses every kind of workshop joinery. Take a look at our site and feel free to contact us at anytime.
